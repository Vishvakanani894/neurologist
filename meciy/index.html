About NeuroHaven
About NeuroHaven

NeuroHaven was founded by Gaurav Patil during his 11th grade time when he noticed how many teens around him were silently battling emotional turbulence. He began as a quiet listener, a compassionate peer, eventually building a safe harbor for others to express, heal, and grow.

  • Our Mission

    To create a safe, judgment-free community for teenagers to find emotional support, share their truths, and navigate life’s tides together.

  • Our Vision

    A generation of emotionally resilient youth—empowered, heard, and healed.
